Below are some of my journey in the worship of the Lord

The Trinity

This section studies the scriptures which deal with the Trinity. Meditations and adorations on scriptures with these themes solidify the teachings of the church on the Trinity

To be clothed with Christ

This meditation theme looks at words from our Blessed Savior, to emphasise His Goodness. Just by meditating on His Goodness can we counter all the negativity we are facing in our daily lives in the world.
